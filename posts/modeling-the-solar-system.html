Modeling the Solar System as Documents

Stars, planets, and moons map neatly onto Sanity documents linked by references: a moon orbits a planet, a planet orbits a star. One GROQ query walks those links to render the whole system. The catch learned the hard way: a reference target must be published before another document can point to it.
